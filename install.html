Our Install Process

How It Works:

Pre-install
Pre-install site meeting to ensure the client understands the Lyons safety procedures. Daily safety tailgate meeting amongst crew. Review site logistics, and perform action plan. Meet and exceed production goals for the day.
Installation
Foreman oversees all aspect of construction site. All change order requests by be signed and approved by client prior to moving forward. Pre-plan material and labour logistics for next day.
Completion
Foreman and project manager site walkthrough. Project manager and client site walkthrough. Project completion sign off by client. Final invoice and payment. Option for maintenance, testimonial when desired.

Lyons Difference

Lyons Landscaping has been voted 'Best Landscaper in Kamloops' for 12 years in a row (Kamloops This Week Readers' Choice Award). Our passionate and experienced team of landscapers deliver quality to your project, regardless of size and style.

This past Summer, we worked on a large development project in Vernon named Predator Ridge which included a number of luxury estate homes and constructing a commercial-sized lavender trail. The lavender trail leads to a full size wooden deck covered by a custom shade sail, with a scenic view looking towards Kalamalka Lake - a perfect location for wedding ceremonies and other group functions.

Safety isn't expensive, it's priceless. We follow our daily procedures to ensure that employees work safely and efficiently on every job site.
